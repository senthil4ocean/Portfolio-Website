Professional Profile

Bridging Earth Observation and Geospatial Technology

Based inChennai, Tamil Nadu CurrentlyResearch Scientist · National Centre for Coastal Research, MoES, INDIA

As a Geospatial Scientist with a decade of experience in GIS, remote sensing, and spatial analytics, I specialize in unlocking the power of location intelligence to solve complex environmental, ecological, and land-use challenges. My work bridges the gap between raw data—such as satellite imagery, drone surveys, and field measurements—and the actionable evidence required for sustainable management, climate resilience, and policy enforcement across the broader geospatial domain.

At the core of my expertise is Earth Observation and Spatial Analytics, where I decode how natural and built environments evolve over time. By leveraging multi-decadal satellite data and advanced remote sensing techniques, I provide deep spatial insights for diverse geographical applications. To ensure this science is robust and scalable, I build reproducible spatial workflows using ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, and Python automation. I believe that a well-documented, transparent workflow is just as important as the analysis itself, allowing methodologies to be audited, shared, and trusted across any geospatial project.

My approach is deeply rooted in field-to-finish ground truthing. As a DGCA-certified remote pilot, I lead end-to-end UAV surveys, integrating aerial data with ground measurements and local stakeholder insights. This ground-level context ensures that high-level geospatial models align perfectly with on-the-ground realities, making me highly adaptable to a wide array of terrain and environmental mapping initiatives.

Over my career, I have translated these technical capabilities into high-stakes impact across multiple sectors:

  • Resource & Environmental Planning: Co-authored comprehensive Shoreline Management Plans (SMPs) for four Indian coastal states using the UK-adopted SMP framework, and contributed to Marine Spatial Planning initiatives in West Bengal.
  • Legally Admissible Mapping: Delivered critical spatial analysis and loss-mapping directly utilized by the National Green Tribunal (NGT) for environmental enforcement and litigation.
  • Institutional Leadership: Contributed to landmark government initiatives across the Ministry of Earth Sciences (NCCR) and the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (NCSCM).
  • Academic Foundation: Built a rigorous theoretical background through a 5-year Integrated M.Sc. in Geosciences from Bharathidasan University, highlighted by thesis research at INCOIS focused on ocean state forecast validation.

Beyond the lab and mapping interface, I am a firm believer in open data, public methods, and the quiet usefulness of a well-documented workflow. I bring this same open, educational ethos to my community work as a member of Nedunal, a permaculture outreach program dedicated to fostering environmental education and ecological literacy at the grassroots level. I am eager to apply this versatile geospatial toolkit to new challenges, collaborations, and domains.
